How PTI Clinic Works

  1. Students/staff login with name & role.
  2. They book appointments or chat with Aisha.
  3. Nurse checks vitals (optional).
  4. Doctors prescribe drugs.
  5. Pharmacists dispense them.
  6. Admin monitors activities.
