How PTI Clinic Works

  1. Students and staff log in using their name and role (no registration).
  2. Students book appointments or chat with Aisha the assistant.
  3. Nurse (optional) records vitals like BP and temperature.
  4. Doctors view files and prescribe medication.
  5. Pharmacists dispense the drugs and mark them as given.
  6. Admin can monitor all user activities and reports.
